Frequently Asked Questions

What is ARANYA and what does it aim to do?

ARANYA is a wildlife welfare initiative focused on rescuing, rehabilitating, and providing lifelong care to animals in need.

Where is ARANYA located?

ARANYA is based in India and serves nearby regions by offering shelter, medical aid, and rehabilitation for rescued animals.

Can I visit or volunteer?

Yes, visitors and volunteers are welcome with prior registration. Please contact us using the form above.

How can I support ARANYA?

You can support ARANYA through donations, adoption, volunteering, or spreading awareness about animal welfare.
